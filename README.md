## What real DJs use every weekend

- Serato DJ Pro – the gold standard for club & mobile DJs  
- Rekordbox DJ – native Pioneer ecosystem, perfect for CDJs  
- Stems 2.0 (Serato) + Stem Split (Rekordbox) – live remixing on the fly  
- Flip, Pitch ’n Time, Expansion Packs  

This community toolkit gives you **ready-to-play packs** that top DJs actually use on stage.

---

## What's inside (drop and rock)

- `Stem Packs/` – 1000+ tracks with separated drums/vocal/bass/melody  
- `Remix Packs/` – official-style edits, acapellas, intros/outros  
- `DJ Drops & Tags/` – voice tags, sweepers, airhorns, sirens  
- `FX Chains/` – echo outs, filter risers, beat rolls, reverb throws  
- `Performance Templates/` – pre-mapped cue points, loops, hot cues  
- `Controller Mappings/` – DDJ-1000SRT, FLX10, RZ, OPUS-QUAD, XDJ-XZ  
- `Sample Packs/` – vocal chops, drum hits, build-ups, impacts  
- `Serato Playlists/` – curated crates (House, Hip-Hop, Techno, Latin, EDM)

## Installation (macOS) – 60 seconds

1. Click the button →downloads  
2. Drag packs into Serato “_Serato_” folder or Rekordbox “Pioneer” folder  
3. Restart app → everything appears instantly
